Ethan Whitney Backyard Concert

December 06, 2024

Whitney backyard stage, Paradise Valley, AZ

Zoe Nichols performed solo as Zoe Be with beautiful songs "Ode to Mo," "It's Not My Mess Too", and "Sylvie's Song", as well as a cover of The Police's "Walking on the Moon". She then accompanied country music artist Elle Sloan on acoustic guitar, and did likewise for singer and keyboard player Sam Kessler.

Cooper Nichols joined Elle Sloan's band on drums as well.
