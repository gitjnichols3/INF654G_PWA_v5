Porch Rock Backyard Concert

April 13, 2024

Nichols backyard stage, Peoria, AZ

Porch Rock performed their debut public show at the Nichols Backyard stage in April. Band members include Riley Blocker, Zoe Nichols, Brea Stephens, Sky Prikey and Hannah Warcola. Zoe also alternated with solo material for a very fun night under the stars.
