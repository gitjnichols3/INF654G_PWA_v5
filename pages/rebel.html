Right Rosemary

November 11, 2024

The Rebel Lounge, Phoenix, AZ

Zoe Nichols sang backup vocals with band Right Rosemary at The Rebel Lounge. Band members include Zoe, Riley Blocker, Kyler Paden, Jules Schubert and Blake Manke.

Zoe then went on to accompany artist friend Sam Kessler on acoustic guitar for his set as well.
