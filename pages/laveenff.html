Laveen Folk Festival

November 23, 2024

Laveen, AZ

Zoe Nichols performed a solo set at the Behind the Barn stage at the Laveen Folk Music Festival. She garnered much attention including a four-legged fan that wanted to join in on the act.

Later, Zoe's band Porch Rock, played a set at the Horse Stage featuring Riley Blocker, Zoe Nichols, Brea Stephens, Sky Prikey and Hannah Warcola. Multiple band members stuck around to participate in the annual impromptu band scramble competition. Zoe and Sky's band won second place!
